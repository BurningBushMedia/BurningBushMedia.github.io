About Me



The Burning Bush is one of the most powerful images in all of the religious and literary works in the history of man. The idea of Moses wandering through the desert and coming upon God in the form of a flaming bush that was not consumed by the fire is one that I find myself returning to often. The concept of a wanderer in the desert encountering God and recieving divine inspiration, knowledge, and purpose is a beautiful one.

Burning Bush Media is a repository for many of the things I have created. It serves as both a documentation of my work and as an outlet for the creative inspiration I recieve.



I am a content creator with a focus on TV and film and I am a 2016 graduate of Murray State University with a Bachelor's of Science in Television Production. If you have any questions about my credentials you can take a look at my Resume here.
